## Summary

Anthropic experiment measuring performance gap recovery between weaker and stronger models. Human researchers (7 days) closed 23% of gap. Nine parallel Automated Alignment Researchers (Claude Opus 4.6 + extra tools) closed 97% of the same gap. Score 1.0 = full alignment with model trained on ground-truth labels. Presented as evidence automated AI alignment research can dramatically outpace human researchers in speed and coverage.
