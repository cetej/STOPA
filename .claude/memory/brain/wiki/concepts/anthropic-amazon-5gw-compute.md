# Anthropic-Amazon 5 GW Compute Expansion

**Announced**: April 20, 2026  
**Source**: [@AnthropicAI on X](https://x.com/AnthropicAI/status/2046327624092487688)

## Facts

- Compute secured: **up to 5 GW**
- Rollout: begins Q2 2026
- Q4 2026 target: ~1 GW operational
- Purpose: training **AND** deploying Claude (not just training)

## Scale Context

5 GW at typical data center efficiency (~0.5 GW/EFLOP/s) → approximately **10 EFLOP/s** sustained inference capacity. This is massive inference headroom, not just training capacity.

## Signals

- Frontier model race: positioning for next-generation training runs
- Long-term compute positioning against OpenAI/Google
- Scale of inference capacity suggests Claude usage growth trajectory
- AWS deepening as primary cloud partner (not Azure/GCP)

## STOPA Relevance

Context for capacity planning: Claude API rate limits and pricing may shift significantly as this capacity comes online. The inference focus (not just training) suggests Anthropic is investing in serving current-generation models at scale — relevant for STOPA's API budget and rate limit behavior.
