# HeyGen

> Company building AI avatar and video generation tools; creators of HyperFrames agentic video framework.

## Key Facts

- Originally focused on AI avatar creation to replace cameras (ref: sources/heygen-hyperframes-agentic-video.md)
- Pivoted to motion graphics and B-rolls after learning that avatars alone weren't sufficient (ref: sources/heygen-hyperframes-agentic-video.md)
- Built GSAP-based editable motion graphics into their Video Editor; later adopted HTML+CSS+JS approach for agent-generated content (ref: sources/heygen-hyperframes-agentic-video.md)
- Open-sourced HyperFrames 2026-04-18 under Apache 2.0 (ref: sources/heygen-hyperframes-agentic-video.md)
- Core team: Abhay (@AbhayZala7), Jake (@JakeFromHeyGen), James (@Rames_Jusso), Miguel (@Miguel07Code), Vance Ingalls (ref: sources/heygen-hyperframes-agentic-video.md)
