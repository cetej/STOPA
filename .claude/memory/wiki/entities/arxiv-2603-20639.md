# arXiv:2603.20639 — Agentic AI and the Next Intelligence Explosion

> 4-page Science commentary (Vol. 391, eaeg1895, March 21, 2026) arguing that intelligence explosion will be plural/social/distributed, not monolithic; RLHF insufficient for governance of such systems.

## Key Facts

- Authors: Evans J., Bratton B., Agüera y Arcas B. (Google) (ref: sources/agentic-ai-research.md)
- Position paper in Science — no new experiments; empirical basis is Kim et al. arXiv:2601.10825 (ref: sources/agentic-ai-research.md)
- Two theses: (1) intelligence explosion = plural emergent configurations; (2) institutional alignment > RLHF (ref: sources/agentic-ai-research.md)
- Explicitly cites Tomasello's "cultural ratchet" — knowledge accumulation across generations (ref: sources/agentic-ai-research.md)
- Limitation: no mathematical framework, no engineering instructions — "flat town hall transcript" as current limit (ref: sources/agentic-ai-research.md)

## Relevance to STOPA

Conceptual validation of STOPA's multi-agent architecture. Key insight: structured disagreement is a feature, not a bug. Heterogeneity > monoculture in agent teams.
