# MorfFlex CZ

> Czech morphological dictionary with 126.9M lemma-tag-wordform triples, published by ÚFAL Charles University.

## Key Facts

- MorfFlex CZ 2.1: 126,906,921 triples; download `czech-morfflex-2.1.tsv.xz` — 238 MB compressed, 6 GB uncompressed (ref: sources/czech-lexical-resources-research.md)
- License: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 — **non-commercial only** (ref: sources/czech-lexical-resources-research.md)
- Format: TSV (lemma | positional-tag | wordform), UTF-8 (ref: sources/czech-lexical-resources-research.md)
- Released December 2024; hosted at LINDAT `hdl.handle.net/11234/1-5833` (ref: sources/czech-lexical-resources-research.md)
- No definitions — morphological data only (ref: sources/czech-lexical-resources-research.md)

## Relevance to STOPA

Used in ngm-terminology for Czech word form generation and morphological analysis. The non-commercial license blocks commercial use of the data but analysis/research use is permitted. Most comprehensive Czech morphological resource available.
