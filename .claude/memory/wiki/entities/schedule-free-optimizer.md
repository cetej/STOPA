# Schedule-Free Optimizer

> Learning-rate-free training method (arXiv:2405.15682, Defazio et al.) that eliminates the need for any LR schedule; won MLCommons AlgoPerf 2024.

## Key Facts

- Eliminates LR schedule as hyperparameter entirely (ref: sources/gradient-descent-research.md)
- Won MLCommons AlgoPerf 2024 competition (ref: sources/gradient-descent-research.md)
- By same authors as D-Adaptation and Prodigy (Defazio & Mishchenko) (ref: sources/gradient-descent-research.md)
- arXiv:2405.15682 (ref: sources/gradient-descent-research.md)

## Relevance to STOPA

Reference for ML projects (NG-ROBOT, POLYBOT, ORAKULUM) where LR tuning is a barrier. Recommend Schedule-Free or Prodigy when simplicity matters over maximum performance.
