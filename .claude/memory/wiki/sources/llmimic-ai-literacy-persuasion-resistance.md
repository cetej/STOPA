# LLMimic — Experiential AI Literacy Training

> **TL;DR**: Interactive gamified tutorial where users role-play as LLMs through pretraining/SFT/RLHF pipeline. RCT (N=274) shows significant AI literacy improvement (p<.001) and reduced persuasion success (p<.05). Only consumer-side intervention with peer-reviewed experimental evidence against conversational AI persuasion.

## Key Claims

1. LLMimic significantly improves AI literacy (p<.001) and reduces persuasion success (p<.05) — `[verified]` (RCT)
2. Experiential role-playing is more effective than passive education (control = AI history video) — `[verified]` (RCT)
3. Scalable human-centered approach outperforms passive mitigations (labels, AI detectors, disclaimers) — `[argued]`
